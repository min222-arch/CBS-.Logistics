About CBS Logistics Limited

CBS Logistics Limited was established in 2019 as a logistics company catering to the needs of growers who needed their produce to get to the markets in good time and in the right condition offering cold chain refrigeration solutions. We have since then opened different arms as we are committed to optimizing the fresh produce industry and other agricultural value chains to create wealth for our growers. We bring our expertise in production, processing and value addition and logistics easing the players processes in the fields. .

Our Farm to Fork models are curated to support our growers with technical support and automating their farms to produce quality produce,reduce the post-harvest losses by having the best state of the art cold chain storage facilities and processing units to process the produce. Our experts in agronomy,engineering ,biotechnology,environmental experts work together to ensure that farmers are well supported to maximise their production and maintain the environment.

We are keen on sustainable farming and our reforestation team is always supplying our growers with indigenous trees to plant in our farms to maintain a low carbon footprint in our ecosystem.

Mission

At CBS Logistics we aim to intertwin all our teams of experts from our different arms ensuring production maintains good agricultural practices that are safe and up to par with the Global markets as we export our produce all over the world. Our state-of-the-art packhouses are ISO.. approved ,HACCP ensuring we have minimal post harvest losses etc (will clarify on this). Our Logistics and forwarding teams ensure that our clients receive their consignments in good time and in the right conditions..

Vision

Our vision is to ensure African growers both small and large scale growers are maximising the use of their land and increase the social economic impact of growers one farm at a time..

Tea Export

CBS Logistics Limited provides end-to-end tea export solutions, connecting Kenyan tea producers to global markets with efficiency, compliance, and quality assurance at the core. We handle the sourcing, grading, packaging, documentation, and international logistics of premium Kenyan teas, ensuring strict adherence to destination market standards. Our export operations are designed to guarantee product traceability, consistent quality, and timely delivery under FOB and CIF terms, enabling our partners to access reliable, transparent, and sustainable tea supply chains worldwide
